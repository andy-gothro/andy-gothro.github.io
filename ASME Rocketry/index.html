ASME Rocketry

I am the Integration and Design lead on Gonzaga University's ASME Rocketry team, and here is a smal portion of the work I've done for the club during my time here.

I manage the process of combining all of our different subsystems into the rocket, mainly using Openrocket, an open source software for rocket design. Here is an work-in-progress screenshot of our rocket:

The rocket will fly to 10,000ft carrying an 8.8lb dummy payload on a commercially available motor, to reduce complexity as this is our first time launching a rocket at this competition.

We will be competing at the Spaceport America Cup in New Mexico this June and you can follow our progress on our Instagram.
