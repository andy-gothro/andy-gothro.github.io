3D-Printing

My 3D-Printing experience

I have been working with 3D-printers for 6 years, and I am familiar with design specifically for additive manufacturing. I own my own 3D-printer which enables me to print both functional parts and things for fun. I am a big fan of rapid prototyping and iterative design. My senior design capstone project is a 5-axis capable, nonplanar 3d printer, enabling more complex and mechanically strong manufacturing with 3d printers.
